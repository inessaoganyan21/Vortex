About us

Our company is built by people, with working experience in multiple international and local companies in Georgia, and countries of EU. We have been working for quality, efficiency, and outstanding results. In 2018 we decided to create our own company, which would be based on integrity, highest work ethics and standards, would be concentrated on delivering results, take full responsibility on work performed, would be honest with customers, and along with these, would contribute to Georgia’s development towards ecological sustainability. Thus, we created water, wastewater engineering and analytics company, VORTEX Water Engineering. Since its inception VORTEX has gained multiple leading companies as its customers, some of them are Coca Cola, McDonalds, Group Cobra, Argo brewery, EFES brewery, etc…

With our international working and educational backgrounds, through commitment and relentless dedication, via transferring knowledge and technologies from our partner companies from Germany, Italy, and USA, our ambition is to raise the bar on Georgian market towards creating water quality awareness for municipal, businesses and private users.
