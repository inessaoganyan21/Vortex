CPS 3-10
CPS 3-10 is an electronic device capable of changing the frequency of a pump. Integrated directly on the motor, it allows adjusting its speed to supply the same pressure at all times, even when the water demand changes. The CPS powers the electric the motor of the pump on which it is mounted and, by means of a feedback a system that uses a pressure transducer, it changes the supply frequency of the electric motor depending on the water demand in order to keep the output the pressure of the pump constant, the value of which is set on the device (setpoint).

The PVM, PVMI and PVMX
The PVM, PVMI and PVMX are the non-self-priming vertical multistage pump of in-line design, flange or with Victual coupling with equally sized suction and discharge ports. Stage construction with stainless steel impellers, chambers and pressure casing. Pump stub shaft and the motor shaft of the IEC-standards motor are directly close coupled. All pumps are equipped with high-efficiency motors (IE3) and with a cartridge type mechanical seal for easy maintenance. PVM, PVMI and PVMX pumps have different pump sizes and various numbers of stages to provide the flow and the pressure required.
