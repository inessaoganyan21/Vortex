ARGO
VORTEX selected, delivered, and successfully commissioned Ultrafiltration system for one of the oldest and outstanding with its quality, Georgian brewery ARGO.From a source water, contaminated with high amount of silt, which made it impossible to measure SDI (silt density index), we turned the SDI index into 1.1, in combination with minimum hourly supply of water of 40 m3/h, versus promised amount 32 m3/h. So, we exceeded all promises and expectations, in terms of SDI with 300 per cent, and in terms of water quantity with 31 per cent

2019-12

Group Cobra
VORTEX offered complete engineering services to Cobra. Our task was to find a solution to long-term standing problem of measuring turbidity in incoming potable water.VORTEX found a reason for impossibility of measuring turbidity, designed custom engineering solution, installed and successfully commissioned the system. The system works fully in automatic mode, measures turbidity of water with at least 95 per cent precision, and automatically controls condition of the sensor, using Prognosys maintenance module installed. Based on the value of turbidity of water, using sensor Solitax SC, paired with SC1000 Controller by Hach, water pressurization / filtration plant is altering modes of operation, from filtering of water if turbidity level is satisfactory, or discharging highly turbid water into a river.
